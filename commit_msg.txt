Initial commit: 汉家江湖 Godot 4.x project

Core game systems:
- Combat: turn-based tactical with grid movement, qi/rage/timestamp
- Characters: 50+ with potential breakthrough, talents
- Wuxue: 100+ martial arts with effects system
- Xinfa: heart methods with 6-slot qi value system
- Equipment: 10 slots, enhance/refine/wanlian/set bonuses
- Sects: 9 factions with unique mechanics
- Story: branching choices, world events
- World: areas, NPCs, exploration
- PvP: ranked ladder with seasons
- Guild: buildings, tech, secret realm, war
- UI: panels for all major systems
- Character customization: face/body/voice
